Thank a creator

People create amazing things. Make it a habit to thank them - it's easy:
  1. Pick a creation you are thankful for (a book, game, song, tool, anything)
  2. Find out who created it (try to find an actual person)
  3. Write them a thank you and tell them how their creation helped you!
  4. (If the creation is free consider donating/buying merch)
  5. (Repeat regularly)
Bonus points if you praise publically using the hashtag #thankacreator to spread awareness ;).
